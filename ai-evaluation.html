AI Tool Evaluation

Lab 2 | Exploring AI Capabilities and Limitations

AI Tools I Use Daily

My primary experience with AI tools comes from code assistance. I regularly use Claude Code through two different interfaces: Cursor and Zed. These tools have fundamentally changed how I approach coding tasks, from writing boilerplate to debugging complex issues.

These tools excel at understanding context, generating code that follows existing patterns in a project, and explaining complex concepts. However, they work best when given clear, specific prompts and when the user can verify the output.

Exploring Image and Video AI

While I'm comfortable with code-focused AI tools, I have much less experience with image and video generation. This lab provided an opportunity to explore these tools and evaluate their current capabilities.

Early Attempts: DALL-E (2023)

My first experience with image generation came from DALL-E in 2023. The results were... interesting, but clearly showed the limitations of the technology at that time.
